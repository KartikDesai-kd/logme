How many times have you been working out?

When did you take your last medication?

So many things going on in your life that you might want to keep tabs on.

But the hassle in making a note of every activity is just too much.

trakit.me addresses the problem by allowing you to track any activity with one single click of a button.

You create a list of activities you want to track.

When it happens, you open trakit.me on your phone or computer and just hit the log button against that activity.

The activity and the day and time it happened is logged.

The idea is to make it as simple and fast as possible to log it.

Later, you can view your logs whenever you want to. It will always be there.
